**Sales Analysis Dashboard – Project Summary**

This Excel dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across multiple dimensions, enabling better business insights and decision-making.

**Key Highlights:**

**Overall Performance Metrics:**
Total Revenue: ₹3.52M+
Total Orders: 1000
Average Revenue per Order: ₹3,520.98
Average Delivery Time Difference: 5.53 days
**Time-Based Analysis:**
Monthly sales show strong peaks in February and August, indicating seasonal demand trends.
Revenue distribution across days reveals Tuesday and Sunday as the highest-performing days.
**Category Insights:**
Top revenue-generating categories include Colors, Soft Toys, and Sweets.
Lower-performing categories like Mugs and Plants highlight potential growth areas.
**Occasion-Based Sales:**
Highest revenue is driven by Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan occasions.
Occasions like Diwali and Valentine’s Day show comparatively lower performance.
**Geographical Performance:**
Cities like Imphal, Dhanbad, and Kavali lead in order volume.
Provides visibility into regional demand patterns.
**Product Performance:**
Top-selling products include Magnam Set and Quia Gift, contributing significantly to revenue.
**Interactivity:**
Dynamic filters for Occasion, Order Date, and Delivery Date allow users to drill down into specific segments for deeper insights.
**Conclusion:**

This dashboard effectively transforms raw sales data into actionable insights, helping identify high-performing products, peak sales periods, and key customer occasions. It supports data-driven decisions for improving sales strategy, inventory planning, and targeted marketing.
